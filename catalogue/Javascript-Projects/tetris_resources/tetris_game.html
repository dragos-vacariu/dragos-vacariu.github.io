Tetris is a classic game in the player must move and rotate falling bricks of various shapes to complete horizontal rows. When a row is fully filled, it disappears—awarding points and creating more space to continue stacking.

The game ends when the stacked bricks reach the top of the grid and no more moves are possible.

Controls:
  • Use the Left and Right buttons on the screen, or the ← / → arrow keys on your keyboard to move the bricks horizontally.
  • Press the Spacebar or click the Rotate button to rotate the brick.
  • Press the ↓ Down Arrow to accelerate the brick’s fall.
