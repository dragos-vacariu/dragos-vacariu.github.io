Classic Chess is a faithful recreation of the traditional chess game, built entirely in JavaScript. Players compete against an AI opponent, following the official rules of chess — including castling, pawn promotion, check, and checkmate.

Gameplay Features: Human vs AI: The player always controls the white pieces, while the AI manages the black pieces. Rule-complete: Includes all standard chess rules, such as castling, en passant, and pawn promotion (automatic promotion to queen). Legal Move Highlighting: When a piece is selected, all valid moves are highlighted to guide the player. Turn-based Play: Players select a piece, then choose a legal square to move, ending their turn.

How to Play:
  • Click on a square containing your piece.
  • Available legal moves for that piece will be highlighted.
  • Click on your chosen square to move the piece and complete your turn.
  • Continue alternating turns until one player captures the opponent’s king, resulting in checkmate.

Development Notes: This project demonstrates full game logic, including piece movement, check/checkmate detection, castling, and pawn promotion. The AI currently uses a basic move-selection algorithm, providing a playable opponent for single-player mode.
